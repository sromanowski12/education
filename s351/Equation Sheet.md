### **Mean**
- **Population**: $\mu=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/n-1$
- **Sample**: $(\bar{x})$

### **Standard Deviation**
- **Population**: $\sigma=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/N$
- **Sample**: $s=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/n-1$

### **Standard Variance**
- **Population**: $\sigma^2$
- **Sample**: $s^2$
