### _**Measures of Central Tendency**_
1. **Median**
2. **Mode**
3. **Mean**

### _**Mean**_
- **Population**: $\mu=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/n-1$
- **Sample**: $(\bar{x})$

### _**Standard Deviance**_
- **Population**: $\sigma=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/N$
- **Sample**: $s=\sqrt{(\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}/n-1$

### _**Standard Variance**_
- **Population**: $\sigma^2$
- **Sample**: $s^2$
